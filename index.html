Quantum-Enhanced AEM Electrolyzer Optimization System

Advanced quantum-swarm intelligence system for optimizing AEM electrolyzer performance, durability, and yield through real-time adaptive control.

This project addresses critical deficiencies in AEM electrolyzer technology including long-term membrane durability, stack degradation under variable load cycles, and limitations of traditional reactive SCADA frameworks.

The solution implements quantum "swarm intelligence" allowing thousands of networked AEM units to continuously learn from one another, optimizing operating parameters in real-time to extend component life and maximize hydrogen yield.
